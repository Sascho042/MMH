About me

I am a Mechanical Engineer from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh, currently working in R&D - Digital & Circular Technology at Indetechs Software Limited, where I develop Digital Battery Passports (DBP) under the EU Battery Regulation and conduct cradle-to-grave Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) studies using OpenLCA. Previously at BanglaCAT (Caterpillar Inc.), I built Power BI dashboards for supply chain visibility and applied Python-based analytics for demand forecasting.

My undergraduate research included fabricating centrifugal pump impellers across varying blade geometries using FDM additive manufacturing to study efficiency relationships, and experimental fatigue testing of notched aluminium specimens under rotary bending to generate S-N curves and quantify stress concentration effects. I have co-authored two conference papers and a journal paper currently under review, and have served as peer reviewer for three international IEOM conferences including the 2nd IEOM World Congress, Windsor (2025).

Beyond my research, technical skills include Ansys, Abaqus, MatLab and many more.

I am currently seeking MS/PhD positions for Spring or Fall 2027 in research areas spanning Industry 5.0, additive manufacturing, LCA, fatigue mechanics, sustainable battery technology and circular economy systems.
